All the programs normally follow the following route as stated below . There will be :

  • One Inline Test after every 4 Modules.
  • One Mid Term exam after completion of 50% of the course.
  • One Final Exam after completing all the modules and tests attached.

People who score more than 75% in the Participation certificate will be eligible for taking the Professional Certificate which will be awarded via an Onsite Proctored Exam at QCI defined centres. There may be a attached workshop/s depending on course needs as well.
